About Me

I'm a STEM high school student who likes coding and solving problems. I built STEMO TRACKER to help myself and other students keep track of how long we actually spend studying — because I kept losing track of time!

Why I Built This

Most timers I found online are too simple — they don't let you label what you're studying or save your sessions. I wanted something that actually helps you see where your study time is going, so I made my own.
